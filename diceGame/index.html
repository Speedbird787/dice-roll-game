Instructions

This is a 2 player dice roll game. In each turn, a player can roll the dice multiple times and their result gets added to the current score; BUT, if a player rolls a 1, their whole current score gets lost and it will be the other player's turn. The player can choose to 'Bank', which means that their current score gets added to their total score. After that, it's the next player's turn; you can agree a final score that you both would like to play to by entering it below and the player to reach it first wins! Good luck.
